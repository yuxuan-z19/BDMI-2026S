# Day 10 Reflection — Speech Recognition (ASR)
Au Yi Teng (GHC)

## Summary
Introduction to Audio processing and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) using the TorchAudio library.

## What I Learned
- ASR Basics: The pipeline for converting acoustic signals into text.
- TorchAudio: Handling waveforms, spectrograms, and Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients (MFCCs).
- Feature Extraction: How machines "hear" audio through frequency analysis.

## Reflection
It’s impressive how similar the pipeline becomes to Computer Vision once you convert audio into a visual representation like a spectrogram.

## Challenges
- Dealing with the inherent "noise" and environmental variability in audio data.
- Understanding the complexity of spectrogram parameters like window size and hop length.
